How does it work?

Naloxone Hydrochloride is an opioid antagonist which works by blocking opioid receptors in the body. This can reverse CNS depression including depressed breathing, extreme drowsiness and loss of consciousness.

How is it used?

Narcan is the most common brand of Naloxone, and Nasal spray is the most common form used. The dosage for adults and children of any age is one spray in each nostril which delivers 4mg of naloxone. Guide

Any side-effects?

After Naloxone is used to reverse an overdose, essentially, they will begin withdrawl immediatly. Common side effects are:

  • Feeling Nervous, restlessness, irritability
  • Body Aches
  • Dizziness or weakness
  • Fever, chills

    Introducing Naloxone
    (Naloxone hydrochloride)

    Naloxone is a life-saving medication that can reverse an overdose from opioids, including heroin, fentanyl, and prescription opioid medications.

    Over 27,000 lives have been saved since 1999 as a result of Narcan(Naloxone) kits given to friends and family to reverse opioid overdoses
